Hongzhi Emperor

A Man of Great Stature

Portrait of the middle-aged emperor of Ming Dynasty China

  • Born in Ming Dynasty 30 July 1470 to Chenghua Emperor and Consort Ji
  • 1475 - Reunited with father after being secretly raised away from his eyes in fear of upsetting the current favored consort
  • 1487 - Marries future Empress Zhang, and later that year ascended the throne of the Ming Dynasty, He is one of the only emperors in Chinese history to remain monogamous during his reign
  • (1487 - 1505) - Emperor Hongzhi promotes confucianism andenacts economic reform
  • (1487 - 1505) - The use of glazed pottery started to become mainstream during Honzhi Emperor's reign
  • 1505 - Dies at the age of 34, leaving the Ming Dynasty to his crown prince
